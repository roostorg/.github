**We’re [ROOST] (Robust Open Online Safety Tools), a non-profit that builds, releases, and maintains modular, open-source infrastructure for online safety.** We serve product teams, trust & safety leads, and community moderators who need to review, triage, and enforce on harmful content without building everything from scratch or relying on opaque or expensive vendor tools. We focus on modular, self-hostable, and interoperable components.
